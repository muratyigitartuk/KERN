Service policy summary

- Standard outbound shipments for domestic business customers leave every Tuesday and Friday.
- Orders approved before 15 April 2026 may still use the older packaging terms.
- Delivery dates may only be confirmed when stock and carrier capacity are both marked as available in the current order sheet.
- If capacity is not documented, KERN should say that the date cannot be confirmed from the available material.

Notes:
- This document does not contain an order-specific stock confirmation.
- This document does not state that shipment on 18 April 2026 is guaranteed.
